5-12-19 ihop

i took my mom and brother to ihop for mothers day, it was an alright time

My brother and i got the brunch burger while mom had a double blueberry stack

My brother's quote is that the burger was "pretty good, i think i put too much pepper on the egg though"

My mom had this to say about the double blueberry stack "it was super good, however it was too much for me to eat, good thing ihop has takeout boxes!"

For my encounter, the eggs were a bit overcooked, and the hashbrowns were a bit too crispy, the patty makes up for it in flavor and heartiness
